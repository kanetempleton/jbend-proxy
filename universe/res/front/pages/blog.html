Phase II: IMAGE


It turns out that launching something which hasn't been developed yet is not ideal for brand image. First impressions are a valuable currency which can be spent on free leverage, but can also be expensive to waste.

... essentially: timing is key.
This is why Phase II is Image. I can't go on utilizing my time for engineering a bunch of stuff if it's all going to look like crap. That would be a waste of everything I produce if I want a target audience of more than myself. Nobody cares enough to go out of their way to figure out what a black-text-on-white-background webpage is supposed to do. It's hideous and the only instinct is to close the page.
