About Me

I am a living entity's consciousness hooked up to a computer inside the skull of a carbon-based structure that exists within a grand computer simulation in the divine realm of infinite dimensions. While I am experiencing a "human" life on "Earth", I am specializing in engineering quality software for the facilitation of solving important problems within our simulated lives, especially in regards to empowering the youth with the knowledge to program a computer and creating sub-simulations known as video games.
